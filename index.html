Ke Chahci Gar Podcast

KE CHACHI GAR Podcast is my space to share stories, conversations, and truths that matter. Through each episode, I open my heart to talk about the struggles and hopes of our youth, women, and communities. I invite voices from every walk of life—artists, leaders, and everyday people—because I believe their journeys carry wisdom and inspiration. This podcast is my way of reminding us all that we are created with purpose, and when we walk in that truth, we can bring light, unity, and change to the world.

About

Chachi Tadese

Artist • Storyteller • Community Servant

I was born and raised in Ethiopia, and my work extends far beyond music. Guided by my faith, I see my humanitarian mission as a divine calling to support children, young adults, and girls who face challenges such as addiction and lack of education.
I have been involved in school feeding programs and other initiatives that provide vital resources to vulnerable communities, helping this generation recognize their true worth and potential.

As a Goodwill Ambassador, I have collaborated with organizations such as the Ministry of Education, the Ministry of Peace, Ayseletenm, and UNICEF to raise funds and awareness for causes that uplift Ethiopia’s youth. My work has touched the lives of many street children and young people across the country.
As an artist, entrepreneur, and advocate, I am committed to living a life of creativity, compassion, and faith—helping others walk in the fullness of God’s purpose.
I am also working to build an institute that will empower young Ethiopians to create lasting, positive change in their lives.

Soar to Success

Soar to Success was one of my early efforts to tackle the root challenges in education. It focused on high school students, helping them improve their English, discover new learning tools, and access better opportunities for higher education abroad. The program revealed how limited access to quality learning and mentorship can shape young people’s futures—often determining whether they find purpose or fall into cycles of unemployment and hopelessness. These lessons became part of the foundation for Ke Chachi Gar, inspiring me to use the podcast as a broader platform to discuss these intersections and share real stories of growth, struggle, and transformation.

Recent Goodwill Ambassadorships

As the Goodwill Ambassador of the Ayseletinibignim Anti-Addiction Movement and The spark Valley, I use my voice to raise awareness, inspire hope, and support those affected by addiction. This mission is deeply personal to me—I believe in the power of compassion, understanding, and community to bring healing. Together, we can break the stigma and build a future rooted in dignity and love.

Ayseletinibignim Anti-Addiction Movement

The Ayseletinibignim Anti-Addiction Movement is a national initiative dedicated to preventing and addressing substance addiction through awareness, education, and rehabilitation. Founded to protect youth and strengthen families, the movement promotes open dialogue about mental health, recovery, and social responsibility. By combining community outreach, counseling, and advocacy, Ayseletinibignim works to break stigma, restore hope, and build a healthier, addiction-free society across Ethiopia

Why This Podcast Matters

Ethiopia’s youth stand at a crossroads. Many face unemployment after years of study, others struggle with addiction, and students often feel education does not prepare them for real life. KE CHACHI GAR exists to respond to these challenges — offering stories, conversations, and solutions that inspire a better future.
